Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving forward with groundbreaking ideas and cutting-edge technology, Bangladesh 2.0 fosters a culture of creativity and entrepreneurship to build a resilient and competitive economy for the future.

Sustainability

Committed to preserving the environment, Bangladesh 2.0 focuses on renewable energy, sustainable practices, and eco-friendly solutions to ensure a greener and healthier nation for generations to come.

Infrastructure

Transforming the nation with modern infrastructure, Bangladesh 2.0 invests in advanced transportation networks, smart cities, and robust systems that support a rapidly growing population and economy.

Empowerment

Empowering communities with education, equal opportunities, and inclusive growth, Bangladesh 2.0 aims to uplift every citizen to contribute meaningfully to the nation's progress.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to service for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

The only place where poverty should be is in museums. We have to get out of this mindset that the rich will do the business and the poor will have the charity. I strongly believe that we can create a poverty-free world, if we want to. In that kind of world, the only place you can see poverty is in the museum. When school children will be on a tour of the poverty museum, they will be horrified to see the misery and indignity of human beings. They will blame their forefathers for tolerating this inhuman condition to continue in a massive way.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Advisor of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
